Iran: is a country located in western Asia. It is the third largest country in the Middle East in terms of population after Egypt and Turkey, with a total of 81 million people, as well as the second largest country in the Middle East, after Saudi Arabia, with an area of ​​1,648,195 km² Iran is distinguished by a geopolitical location that makes it a meeting point for three Asian areas (West, Central and South Asia). It is bordered on the north by Armenia, Azerbaijan and Turkmenistan. Iran overlooks the Caspian Sea (an inland sea bordered by Kazakhstan and Russia). It is bordered on the east by Afghanistan and Pakistan, on the south by the Persian Gulf and the Gulf of Oman, on the west by Iraq and on the northwest by Turkey. Tehran is the capital, the largest city in the country and the political, cultural, commercial and industrial center of the nation. Iran is a regional power, and it occupies an important position in international energy security and the global economy due to its large reserves of oil and natural gas. Iran has the second largest natural gas reserves in the world and the fourth largest proven reserves of oil. Iran is home to one of the oldest civilizations in the world. The first dynasty formed in Iran during the Elamite Kingdom in 2800 BC. The Medes united Iran into an empire in 625 BC. Then the Iranian Achaemenid Empire, and then the Hellenic Seleucid Empire and two of the later Iranian empires, the Parthians and Sassanids, were established before the Islamic conquest in 651 AD. And during the dynastic era of the Islamic State. The Persian language and culture spread throughout the Iranian plateau.
