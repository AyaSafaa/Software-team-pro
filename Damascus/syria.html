Syria is an Arab country that is considered a central republic, consisting of 14 governorates, the capital and largest city of which is Damascus. Located within the Middle East region in West Asia; It is bordered to the north by Turkey, to the east by Iraq, to the south by Jordan, to the west by Palestine, Lebanon, and the Mediterranean Sea, with an area of 185,180 square kilometers, and a varied topography and vegetation and animal cover, and the climate ranging between Mediterranean and semi-arid. Syria ranks side by side with Iraq as the oldest sites for the cradle of human civilization.
